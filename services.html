EXPERIENCE DESIGN

Experience Design, often abbreviated as XD, is a multidisciplinary approach that focuses on creating meaningful and memorable experiences for users or customers across various touchpoints. It involves the thoughtful integration of design, technology, psychology, and business strategy to enhance the overall user journey and satisfaction. Whether in the digital realm or physical spaces, Experience Design aims to optimize interactions and evoke positive emotions.
