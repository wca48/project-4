About the Founder Rupal Borge:

VISUALISE CREATE COMMUNICATE

Our founder, Rupal Borge a NIFT Alumni has had a roller coaster of a journey in the Fashion and Lifestyle Industry.

With an experience of 7 years and counting, Rupal started her journey in the creative business at the age of 19 with The Loom as a junior stylist. With an inclination towards running businesses, she started working as a Social Media Strategist and Consultant, post which she ended up consulting brands and homegrown agencies before venturing out on her own.

After consulting and working for some leading names in the Business of Fashion and Lifestyle, she set the foundation for Chakra after understanding a huge gap in the digital landscape for Fashion and Lifestyle within India. With an experience of working with the Ministry of Textiles for multiple cluster projects, Rupal has a thorough understanding of not only the Business of Fashion and Lifestyle, but also an extensive knowledge of the Crafts of India and firmly believes that the Future of India lies in its craft revival.

In her free time, she loves to work for the welfare of Mental Health Awareness and also enjoys mentoring aspiring individuals in the field of Fashion, Design and Marketing.

Chakra is a 360 Marketing and Content Studio Based in India, Deciphering the Power of an Image to You.
